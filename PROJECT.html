Core Multimodal Functionalities

1. The Wellness Index & SOS Trigger

A continuous, holistic score that instantly diagnoses your weakest link. If the Index shows a **continuous slow degrade**, a Trigger Alarm contacts a medical professional.

2. The Analytical AI Counselor

This AI is trained like a mental health doctor. Its response is deep, objective, and based on your specific biometric and burnout data to find the *real* cause.

3. Biometric Data Fusion Engine

Secure API integration (Google Fit, Apple Health, Noise, Boat) streams your vital signs directly into the Fusion Logic engine.

4. Daily Work Burnout Predictor

Proactively analyzes the fusion of daily work activities, sleep hours, and screen time to determine your exact exhaustion level.
