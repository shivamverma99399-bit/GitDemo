The MindCare AI Solution: A Unified Platform

MindCare AI is not just another app. It's an integrated ecosystem designed to proactively manage wellness by connecting the three pillars of health: mental state, burnout, and nutrition. We use on-device AI to provide private, personalized insights and interventions.

Pillar 1: Proactive Burnout Detection

Burnout is more than just feeling tired; it's a syndrome of exhaustion, cynicism, and reduced performance. Our AI models analyze passive data to detect it early, before it becomes a crisis.

AI-Identified Risk Factors

Our system learns an individual's baseline by monitoring key behavioral metrics. It flags deviations in **sleep, screen time, study duration, and mood log sentiment** to predict burnout risk.

Proven Model Accuracy

Research-backed models form our foundation. AI achieves high accuracy in identifying burnout from **textual data (NLP)** and **behavioral patterns (ML)**, ensuring reliable insights.

Pillar 2: Effortless Nutrition Tracking

Good nutrition is the foundation of mental health, but manual logging is tedious. We use cutting-edge computer vision to make tracking as simple as taking a photo, eliminating the #1 barrier to healthy eating.

The Core Engine: The Wellness Index

This is where it all comes together. The Wellness Index is a single, easy-to-understand score (0-100) that provides a holistic view of a user's health by intelligently weighting the three pillars.

Composition of the Index

The Formula for Health

The index is a weighted sum, prioritizing mental state while acknowledging the critical support of physical health and rest.

40% Mental Score

Derived from mood logs, journal sentiment, and chatbot interactions.

30% Physical Score

Based on nutrition balance, exercise frequency, and sleep quality.

30% Burnout Score

Calculated from sleep, screen time, and study duration data (inverted).
